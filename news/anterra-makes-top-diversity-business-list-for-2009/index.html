AnTerra Makes Top Diversity Business List for 2009

Once again AnTerra is proud to announce that this year they have received two distinguishing awards from Diversity Business. “Top 100 Small Businesses in California” and “Top 500 Small Businesses in the United States.”

This classification represents the top small businesses in the U.S., in sectors such as technology, manufacturing, food service and professional services. Large organizational buyers throughtout the country that do business with multicultural, small and women-owned businesses use the list. The winning companies will be honored at a special awards ceremony at Diversity Business’s “9 Annual Multicultural Business Conference”, taking place April 29th-May 1st, 2009 at the Disney’s Contemporary Resort in Orlando, Florida.

March 19, 2009
