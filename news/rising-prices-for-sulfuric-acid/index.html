Rising Prices for Sulfuric Acid

A recent article by Michael Mccoy, C&EN Northeast New Bureau, touches on the current events that are converging to drive up the price of sulfuric acid to record highs. He states that the spot price for sulfuric acid sold on the U.S. Gulf Coast is four times higher today than it was a year ago. Many manufacturing operations rely on this chemical and therefore have a widespread affect on many industrial users. Mccoy states that Robert Boyd, founder of the sulfur and sulfuric acid consulting firm PentaSul., believes that the prices will come down in the long term, but not before 2009
