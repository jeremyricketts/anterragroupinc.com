Water Treatment

Anterra Group’s Water Treatment Division is a leading supplier of water treatment products and process solutions. AnTerra delivers value through integrated comprehensive solutions that improve performance and product quality by reducing operational costs and extending equipment life. These products and services are used to optimize system performance, safeguard customer’s equipment from corrosion, bacterial deposition, scaling and fouling and protect the environment through water and energy conservation.

AnTerra provides products, technical services and training programs for boiler, cooling, membrane filtration and ion exchange systems. AnTerra is the foremost developer and supplier of water treatment products and technologies in the fruit and vegetable canning and processing industries. AnTerra has developed unique processes for inhibition of corrosion and deposition on food cans and canning equipment and treatment of vegetable ﬂume wash water treatment based on numerous microbiological oxidants including chlorine, chlorine dioxide, ozone and peracetic acid. We work closely with our clients to develop programs that will insure maximum return on your water treatment investment and retain optimum efficiency which results in an overall low cost operation.

AnTerra offers monthly programs which include all chemicals, drumless chemical handling and storage, feed and monitoring equipment, inventory management, 24 hour on call emergency service, technical consulting and freight at a guaranteed monthly fixed priced.

As an AnTerra customer, you can rely on a company that keeps the promises others make, while continuing to deliver bottom line profits through proactive customer relations.
