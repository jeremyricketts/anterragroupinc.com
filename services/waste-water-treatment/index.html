Waste Water Treatment

Anterra Group’s Wastewater Treatment Division offers a comprehensive product range of wastewater treatment products, technologies and processes. AnTerra provides wastewater management solutions using the most advanced technologies and quality products which results in truly efficient, cost effective operations. AnTerra has long been recognized for its broad selection of versatile, unique, reliable technologies that maximize water treatment efficiency, reduce operational costs and extending equipment life.

Comprehensive, Compliant and Cost-effective!

AnTerra can provide treatment solutions for a diverse range of both municipal and industrial wastes. AnTerra Group’s main aim in reviewing effluent waste streams is development of environmentally safe recycling processes wherever possible. These products and services are used to optimize system performance, assure state and federal compliance, lower utility costs, and protect the environment through water and energy conservation.

AnTerra provides polymers for coagulants and polymers for wastewater and drinking water, odor control agents, oxidants for microbiological control including chlorine, chlorine dioxide and ozone and a variety of pH and water conditioning chemistries.

AnTerra offers monthly programs which include all chemicals, drumless chemical handling and storage, feed and monitoring equipment, inventory management, 24 hour on call emergency service, technical consulting and freight at a guaranteed monthly fixed priced.

As an AnTerra customer, you can rely on a company that keeps the promises others make, while continuing to deliver bottom line profits through proactive customer relations.
