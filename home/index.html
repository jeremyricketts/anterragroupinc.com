Welcome to AnTerra

AnTerra Group Inc. is an industry leading supplier of innovative products, services and solutions to customers in the food processing, industrial and municipal markets. Headquartered in Laguna Niguel, California, AnTerra also maintains satellite manufacturing facilities in Montana, Arizona, Fresno, Los Angeles and Imperial California. We service customers primarily in the western states, Mexico and Canada.

The AnTerra Difference

AnTerra is a dynamic, fast growing company committed to the success and growth of our customers. At AnTerra we are proud of the way we conduct business. Our dedication to our clients extends far beyond the initial sale with the use of quality management tools, plant audits preformed by experienced technicians, extensive ongoing bilingual training programs and our market specific technical support. While many companies make promises of superior service, “We keep the promises they make”.

Single Source Supplier

AnTerra as a single source supplier can meet your entire requirement for cleaning, sanitation, water treatment and waste water treatment services and products. This integrated approach, can eliminate potential negative product interactions, reduce chemical inventories, provide unified training programs, reduce operational costs and increase profitability.
