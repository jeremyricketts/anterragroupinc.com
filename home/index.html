Welcome to AnTerra

AnTerra Group Inc. is an industry leading supplier of innovative products, services and solutions to customers in the food processing, industrial and municipal markets. Headquartered in Laguna Hills, California, AnTerra also maintains satellite manufacturing facilities in Fresno, Los Angeles and Imperial California. We service customers in most of the western states, Mexico and Canada.

The AnTerra Difference

AnTerra is a dynamic, fast growing company committed to the success and growth of our customers. At AnTerra we are proud of the way we conduct business. Our dedication to our clients extends far beyond the initial sale with the use of quality management tools, plant audits preformed by experienced technicians, extensive ongoing bilingual training programs and our market specific technical support. While many companies make promises of superior service, “We keep the promises they make”.

Single Source Supplier

AnTerra as a single source supplier can meet your entire requirement for cleaning, sanitation, water treatment and waste water treatment services and products. This integrated approach, can eliminate potential negative product interactions, reduce chemical inventories, provide unified training programs, reduce operational costs and increase profitability.

Anterra Group has developed a patented* application method designed to utilize carbon dioxide to optimize chlorine in fruit and vegetable wash systems. Read more about the Genesis CO2 system.

Individual Solutions

At AnTerra we provide individual solutions to meet our customer’s requirements. All product, equipment, process and business development is customer directed. Our company structure allows great flexibility to rapidly respond to the needs of our clients and insure complete customer satisfaction.
